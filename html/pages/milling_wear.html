Milling Wear

The dataset comprises experimental data from milling operations, focusing on the wear of milling inserts at different speeds, feeds, and depths of cut. It's aimed at studying the degradation patterns to predict and understand wear mechanisms.

Dataset Information

Experiments on a milling machine for different speeds, feeds, and depth of cut. Records the wear of the milling insert, VB. The data set was provided by the UC Berkeley Emergent Space Tensegrities (BEST) Lab.
